# IEEE DataPort 数据集申请指南

## 目标数据集

**Real-life Power Quality Sags Dataset**
- **链接**: https://ieee-dataport.org/documents/real-life-power-quality-sags-dataset
- **描述**: 真实环境下采集的电压暂降（voltage sag）数据
- **适用**: 电压异常检测研究

## 申请步骤

### Step 1: 创建 IEEE 账号

1. 访问 https://ieee-dataport.org/
2. 点击 **Sign Up** 注册账号
3. 使用学术邮箱注册可享受更多访问权限

### Step 2: 查找数据集

1. 在搜索框输入 "Real-life Power Quality Sags"
2. 或直接访问上述链接

### Step 3: 提交申请

点击 **Request Access** 按钮，填写以下信息：

---

## 申请模板（英文）

### Research Purpose

```
I am a researcher at [Your Institution] working on rural low-voltage distribution
network voltage anomaly detection. My research focuses on developing deep learning
models (specifically TimesNet-based architectures) for detecting voltage sags,
harmonics, unbalance, and other power quality disturbances.

The Real-life Power Quality Sags Dataset is highly relevant to my research as it
contains real-world voltage sag events that would significantly enhance the
validation of our anomaly detection models. Currently, our research uses synthetic
datasets, and access to real-world data would greatly improve the practical
applicability and credibility of our research outcomes.
```

### Intended Use

```
The dataset will be used for:
1. Training and validating TimesNet-based anomaly detection models
2. Comparative analysis with synthetic data generation methods
3. Evaluating model generalization on real-world power quality events
4. Academic research publication in peer-reviewed journals

The data will be used solely for academic research purposes and will not be
redistributed or used for commercial purposes.
```

### Research Background

```
Research Project: Voltage Anomaly Detection in Rural Low-Voltage Distribution Networks
Institution: [Your Institution]
Principal Investigator: [Your Name]
Duration: [Start Date] - [End Date]

Our project aims to develop intelligent monitoring systems for rural power grids
using time-series deep learning methods. The key innovation is adapting TimesNet
architecture for multi-type voltage anomaly detection with preset electrical
periods (20ms for 50Hz power cycles).
```

## 申请模板（中文，供参考）

### 研究目的

我是[机构名称]的研究人员，正在进行农村低压配电网电压异常检测研究。
我的研究专注于开发基于深度学习的模型（特别是 TimesNet 架构的变体），
用于检测电压暂降、谐波、不平衡等电能质量扰动。

Real-life Power Quality Sags 数据集与我的研究高度相关，因为它包含
真实世界的电压暂降事件，可以显著增强我们异常检测模型的验证效果。
目前我们的研究使用合成数据集，获取真实数据将大大提高研究成果的
实际适用性和可信度。

### 预期用途

该数据集将用于：
1. 训练和验证基于 TimesNet 的异常检测模型
2. 与合成数据生成方法进行对比分析
3. 评估模型在真实电能质量事件上的泛化能力
4. 在同行评审期刊上发表学术研究成果

数据将仅用于学术研究目的，不会重新分发或用于商业用途。

## 注意事项

1. **处理时间**: IEEE DataPort 申请通常需要 1-5 个工作日审核
2. **学术邮箱**: 使用 .edu 或 .ac 邮箱可提高通过率
3. **详细描述**: 申请理由越详细，通过率越高
4. **诚信承诺**: 承诺仅用于学术研究，不商用或转发
